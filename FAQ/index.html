Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
How long will shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within the U.S. and 7-15 days internationally.
Can I return my order?
Yes! Returns are accepted within 30 days. Items must be unworn and unwashed.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer international shipping. Delivery times vary by country.
How can I track my order?
After your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number.
What should I do if I receive a damaged item?
Please contact us immediately at support@cafetana.com. We'll replace or refund your item promptly.
Are your shirts true to size?
Yes, our shirts are designed to fit true to size. Please refer to our sizing chart for exact measurements.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours of placement. Contact us as soon as possible.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! Gift cards can be purchased directly from our website and make the perfect gift.
